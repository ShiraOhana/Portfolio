Hello👋
Welcome to my page!
My name is Shira and I'm from Tel-Aviv, Israel.
I always thought that I would be an Anthropologist and that I would love my job. (Spoiler alert: that didn't happen!)
After graduating from my B.A. degree, I spent four years working in the HR field.
I liked it, but I couldn't imagine myself doing that for the rest of my life.
At the same time, I was working on my thesis for my M.A.
After graduating from my M.A. degree, I started my journey to find a new path for myself.
One day I found a website named - "Shecodes". It was about coding, and motivating women to try it.
I decided to join, and I spent the next 4 months completing tasks, homework, and various challenges.
This piqued my interest in the Tech World and once I tried it, I felt like it was the place for me!
As a result I decided to apply to the "Appleseeds Association" bootcamp and spent the next 6 months learning everything I can about Fullstack development.
